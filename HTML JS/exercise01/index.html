Table of Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity Details (Optional)
1 cup flour -
2 cups sugar brown is preferred
1 cup milk full-cream
1 cup butter -

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, and while it is in the process of reaching that temperature, prepare the ingredients by measuring them out.
  2. Mix all ingredients together using the choice of flask and containers that you have in your kitchen.
  3. Pour all ingredients into a baking dish and place it inside the oven
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
